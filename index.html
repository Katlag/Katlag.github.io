About Us

Moto is a small Wellington based restoration business that specialises in quality paintwork for your veteran, vintage and classic motorcycles – whether British, American, Japanese or European.  We can undertake everything from a simple refresh to a full restoration.

Call me now if you have any queries: 022 524 8059

Tank Sealing

We take pride in our three-step tank sealing service.

Many tanks have been rescued and bought back to use by our tank repair and internal sealing process.  I have repaired tanks with 2 to 103 pinholes.  Please don’t hesitate to contact me with your tank issues, I may be able to help.  I exclusively use Red Kote Fuel Tank Sealer, made in the USA.

Full -or part - Restorations



If you are looking for someone to carry out a restoration of your motorcycle then give me a call to discuss what you are looking for, whether it be a ground-up bare metal restoration, a paint job, or simply a freshen up. We also do patina. I have great contacts with local specialist engineers and platers, we can work with you to bring to life your vision for your motorcycle.

James had shown me some photos of previous work he had completed, the workmanship looked like pure art... and so I entrusted this painting job to him.  All I could provide to him was the raw steel tank and a few fuzzy photographs, all of them in B & W.

Finish coated in what has proven to be a very robust clear lacquer, the tank is truly the crowning glory of the bike’s restoration.
